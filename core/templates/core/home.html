Who We Are

Bihar's Trusted Partner in Digital Transformation

Narasimha Global Service & Solution Pvt. Ltd. (NGS) was founded with a clear mission: to digitize, streamline, and modernize the way government offices and businesses manage their critical records.

Based in Patna, Bihar, our team of trained professionals handles every step of the document digitization lifecycle — from physical scanning and PDF creation to metadata extraction and secure upload to official government portals. We also provide skilled manpower solutions to ensure offices are staffed with qualified personnel.

What We Offer

Our Core Services

From document scanning to government portal integration, we handle the full digital workflow so you don't have to.

🗂️

Document Digitization

High-resolution scanning of legacy government records, registers, land documents, and archival papers with quality verification at every step.

📄

PDF Creation & Archiving

Conversion of scanned images into searchable, indexed PDF files with proper naming conventions, folder structures, and long-term archiving standards.

🔍

Metadata Extraction

Accurate extraction of critical data fields — dates, names, survey numbers, registration details — structured for easy retrieval and government compliance.

🌐

Government Portal Upload

Seamless upload and integration of digitized documents onto official state and central government portals following prescribed data formats.

👥

Skilled Manpower Supply

Deployment of trained data entry operators, scanning technicians, and IT professionals to government offices and private businesses across Bihar.

💻

IT Solutions & Support

Custom software support, hardware setup, network configuration, and ongoing technical assistance tailored to institutional requirements.
